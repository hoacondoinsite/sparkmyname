Common Questions
What do I get with a Consumer report?
You get a full AI-powered name generation session producing 20–50 creative brand name options, live domain availability checking across .com, .net, .io, and .co extensions, and a downloadable branded PDF report. Includes an AI-based trademark risk overview — not a substitute for legal advice.
Is domain availability checking accurate?
Our tool uses live DNS lookup via Cloudflare's DNS-over-HTTPS API to check whether domains currently have active records. This is highly accurate for .com and .net. Always verify with a domain registrar before purchasing. Results are for guidance only.
What's the difference between the Consumer and Professional reports?
The Consumer report gives you names and domain availability. The Professional report adds deep USPTO trademark class analysis, competitor name mapping, brand strength scoring across 6 dimensions, and specific attorney guidance — everything you need before filing a trademark or committing major resources to a brand.
Does this replace a trademark attorney?
No — and we say so clearly on every report. Our AI provides a screening tool to help you eliminate obviously problematic names and focus attorney time on your best candidates. Before adopting any brand name or filing a trademark, always consult a qualified trademark attorney.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, the same payment processor used by Amazon, Google, and Shopify. We never store your credit card information. All transactions are encrypted end-to-end.
