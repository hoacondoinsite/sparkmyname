Last updated: June 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

SparkMyName is operated by Vorrex LLC, a Florida limited liability company. Contact us at supportsparkmyname@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Business description and industry you enter into our name generator

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered brand name suggestions based on your input

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

5. AI Processing

Your business description is sent to an AI language model to generate name suggestions. We do not use your business descriptions to train AI models or share them with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate our platform, including Stripe for payments and Resend for transactional emails.

7. Data Retention

We retain your purchase records for 7 years as required by law. You may request deletion of your personal data by emailing us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at supportsparkmyname@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate our checkout process. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
