Our Policy

SparkMyName delivers digital reports that are generated and available immediately upon payment. Because our reports are delivered instantly and digitally, all sales are final and non-refundable once your report has been generated.

When We Will Make It Right

We stand behind our product. If any of the following occur, contact us within 48 hours and we will either re-generate your report or issue a full refund:

  • You were charged but never received your report due to a technical error
  • You were charged twice for the same report
  • A technical failure prevented your PDF from downloading

What We Cannot Refund

  • Reports that were successfully generated and delivered
  • Dissatisfaction with the AI-generated names (name generation is inherently creative and subjective)
  • Domain availability results (domain status changes in real time)
  • Annual subscriptions after the renewal date has processed

Annual Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your annual subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals. We do not refund partial subscription periods. To cancel, email us at supportsparkmyname@gmail.com.

How to Request Support

Email us at supportsparkmyname@gmail.com with your order details. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
